Instructions

Instructions for Using HealthyBuddy

  • Create an Account: Click on the "SignUp" link in the navigation bar and fill in your personal and medical details.
  • Login: Use your registered email and password to log into your dashboard.
  • Upload Medicine: Navigate to the "Upload Medicine" section and upload a clear image of your medicine. You can also enter the name and dosage manually.
  • Set Reminders: Specify the times and frequency for each medicine. You can also set recurring reminders (e.g., daily, weekly).
  • Track Doses: Check off medicines once taken to keep track of your daily health routine.
  • Manage Profile: Edit your personal information, medical history, and update your emergency contacts from the profile page.
  • Enable Notifications: Allow push or email notifications to receive timely medicine alerts.
  • Access Health Tips: Scroll down to the Health Tips section to read valuable wellness and lifestyle guidance.
  • Need Help? Visit the Contact Us section or email our support team for assistance.
